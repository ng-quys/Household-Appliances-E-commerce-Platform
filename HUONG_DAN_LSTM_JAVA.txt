HUONG DAN TICH HOP LSTM REVENUE FORECAST

Da hoan thanh trong project:
1. Them dependency ONNX Runtime vao pom.xml.
2. Doi thu muc resources/Model thanh resources/ml.
3. Them src/main/java/com/example/WebBanDoGiaDung/service/LstmRevenueModelService.java.
4. Sua RevenueForecastService.java de uu tien du bao bang LSTM ONNX.
5. Sua AdminHomeController.java de truyen forecast7Days, forecast30Days, forecast6Months.
6. Sua templates/admin/home/index.html de hien thi 3 card du bao va bieu do LSTM.

Khi mo project trong IntelliJ:
- Bam Load Maven Changes.
- Chay lai Spring Boot.
- Mo http://localhost:8080/admin/home
- Neu thanh cong console se hien: Da load LSTM revenue model ONNX thanh cong.

Neu khong load duoc model:
- Kiem tra 2 file nam dung tai src/main/resources/ml/revenue_lstm.onnx va src/main/resources/ml/revenue_lstm_meta.json
- Kiem tra Maven da tai duoc com.microsoft.onnxruntime:onnxruntime:1.26.0 chua.
